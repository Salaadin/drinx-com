How Drinx Works

Every month, we select some of the best small batch gins, beers and wines from around Europe, including limited editions not available anywhere else.

You choose how often to receive your box of choice each month - every one, two or three months. You only pay for the months you get and you’re free to swap booze or cancel at any time.

Whats in the box is a surprise until it arrives but always includes a full-sized bottle of craft gin, 12 cans of beer or 6 bottles of fine wine.
